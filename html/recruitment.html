eMigrate System for the Recruitment of Indian Workers

Ministry of Overseas Indian Affairs has launched the eMigrate Project for the recruitment of Indian workers across all the 17 Emigration Check Required (ECR) countries w.e.f 1st June 2015.

All the Foreign Employers, who seeks to recruit Indian workers, need to register in the eMigrate system (www.emigrate.gov.in). The FEs can get registered by logging in to the emigrate website, www.emigrate.gov.in and filing the online application and uploading the required documents.

Once the FE becomes registered in the eMigrate system, the FE can apply for direct recruitment or recruitment through a Recruiting agency in India.
