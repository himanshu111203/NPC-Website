wHo wE aRe?

Libra International Services was established in 2006 under the proprietorship of Dr.Inder Maurya (MD Emergency Medicine). Our day to day operations are run by Mr. Ramkishan Maurya (Director Operations).

Libra International Services is managed by experienced professionals in recruiting manpower for the Gulf countries with its Director operations and Director Business development having extensive experience of working in the Middle East and fully aware of the laws and regulations and what the companies expect when recruiting from India. Our large data bank of professionals and skilled workers in every category is based on interest of the companies requiring variety of manpower.

Starting from initial screening of CVs received from candidates and until the candidates arrival in the Overseas Country we ensure very careful scrutiny and selection at every step of the recruitment process.

Because of its sourcing, processing and deployment capability at the shortest lead time Libra International Services has earned its reputation and has became popular company in Middle East for recruitment services.
